ARCHED OFFICE

Interior Design Project

CA OFFICE

Area : 400 sq m

An Interior Design project based on an indutrial theme in a commercial building in Ahmedabad. The highlight of the project is the use of materials and techniques to achieve a one of a kind design language completely in line with the client requirements.

2

TERRA-KOTA

Residential Design Project and Studio

STUDIO

Area : 340 sq m

Designed for a family of four where the parents would spend their retired life while the children would be engrossed in developing their careers. Fundamentally, it had to respond to the needs of two generations where one can come together to enjoy each other’s company but also respect their time alone. Keeping in mind such a brief the architecture responds to a variety of scales of spaces where one can unwind.

3

HOUSE OF CURVES

Interior Design Project

HOUSE OF CURVES

Area : 4500 sq m

The design mandate was clear: employ a palette of neutral colours, opt for high-quality, natural materials, and create an ambiance that exuded timelessness. Some elements of the existing space were to be retained and thoughtfully integrated, while others were introduced and merged seamlessly to breathe new life into the interiors.

4

OAK HOUSE

Interior Design Project

Residential interiors

Area : 1200 sq m

Oak House is designed for a family of five—a couple, their daughter, and the parents. The brief was clear: minimalistic and timeless interiors that prioritize functionality. The home features earthy tones, oak wood accents, beige hues, and marble flooring in the living and bedroom areas, creating a harmonious and inviting atmosphere.
